#makeyourownartworld + online art curation/distribution

Featured Artists on our Instagram feed

Infinite Industries is a platform, an experiment, a manifesto. It is a way to take ideas behind Contemporary Art and make them vital beyond the traditional boundaries of a luxury goods marketplace! Current technologies call for a revolution in the process of art distribution and education. It is time to move away from complete dependence on the scarcity model and embrace possibilities of a wider dissemination. Our goal is to curate this transition through production of open source software and exhibition experiences in partnership with a number of amazing artists and institutions.

...and for now, it is a work in progress :)
with some very cool projects coming out in the near future.
