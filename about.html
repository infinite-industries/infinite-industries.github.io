Infinite Industries is a platform, an experiment, a manifesto. It is a way to take ideas behind Contemporary Art and make them vital beyond the traditional confines of the luxury goods marketplace! Current technologies call for a revolution in the process of art distribution. It is time to move away from complete dependence on the scarcity model and embrace possibilities of wider dissemination. Our goal is to curate this transition in partnership with a number of amazing artists.
