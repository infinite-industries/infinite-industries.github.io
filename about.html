Infinite Industries is a platform, an experiment, a manifesto. It is a way to take ideas behind Contemporary Art and make them vital beyond the traditional boundaries of a luxury goods marketplace! Current technologies call for a revolution in the process of art distribution and education. It is time to move away from a complete dependence on the scarcity model and embrace possibilities of a wider dissemination. Our goal is to curate this transition through production of open source software and exhibition experiences in partnership with a number of amazing artists and institutions.
